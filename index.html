Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to create an account?

No. FitTrack Pro runs entirely in your browser — open any tool and start using it right away.

Is my data saved anywhere?

Not yet. Each calculator works with the numbers you enter in that session; nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

Are these results medical advice?

No. These tools use standard formulas (like Mifflin–St Jeor for calories) as general estimates. Always consult a professional for personalized guidance.

Is FitTrack Pro really free?

Yes, completely. Every tool is free to use, with no sign-up or subscription required.
